Welcome to Delta Professional Service

Our mission is to deliver services that exceed the expectations of our customers. We plan to do this by creating clean and healthy living and working spaces for our customers and their community while providing clean and safe environments and providing quality and effective cleaning services. We strive to create an environment that is free from germs, bacteria, and other dangerous elements that can cause health issues. We utilize just the most innovative products, the latest technology, and the highest standards of quality. Ultimately, we strive to develop a lasting relationship with our customers by offering exceptional customer service, high standards of cleanliness, and a commitment to protecting your property.

Who are we

Meet The Lopez family from El Salvador. The Lopezs arrived in the United States at the beginning of 2013 looking for new life opportunities and security for their children. They settled in Davis County and founded a small family cleaning business in 2015. Since then, their owners and their children have overseen providing the best professional cleaning service.
